About Me

I am a passionate and dedicated Medical Entomologist with nine years of experience specializing in vector-borne diseases. With a strong foundation in biology, a Master's in Genomics, and a Ph.D. candidacy in Medical and Veterinary Entomology, I am driven to employ innovative and unconventional methods to tackle public health challenges, particularly disease transmission. My expertise spans both fieldwork and laboratory-based experiments, utilizing cutting-edge molecular biology techniques alongside advanced mathematical and statistical modeling. Proficient in R and Python programming, as well as entry to intermediate level in languages as C# and F#, I excel at developing data-driven ready-to-use solutions to complex problems. My research contributions are well-documented through publications in prestigious scientific journals. I am deeply committed to translating knowledge about vector-borne diseases and disease transmission into actionable solutions for decision-makers. My primary professional goal is to develop software and web-based applications that empower public health officials to make informed decisions and effectively combat disease transmission.
